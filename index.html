About the Authors

These are projects by Sanjay and Arvind Seshan from Pittsburgh, PA, USA. We are two middle schoolers who enjoy programming robots. We are the founders of EV3Lessons.com and members of a FIRST LEGO League Team Not the Droids You Are Looking For.
